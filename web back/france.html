Discover French Culture

Art and Architecture

France has a rich tradition of art, with famous artists like Monet and Rodin. The Gothic cathedrals and the Palace of Versailles are architectural marvels.

Festivals and Traditions

Festivals like Bastille Day and Cannes Film Festival are famous worldwide. Traditions include wine and cheese tastings and the art of fashion.
